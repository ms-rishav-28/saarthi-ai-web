The Problem

India's welfare system is broken
— not by design, but by complexity.

There are 1,600+ central schemes and thousands of state-level programs. Most citizens don't know they exist, let alone how to apply. The ones who try face a maze of portals, documents, and offices — all in English.

₹2.5L Cr
Benefits go unclaimed every year
Allocated government welfare budgets remain unspent because eligible citizens never apply.
4.2 hrs
Average time per application
A single PM-JAY pre-authorization takes hours of manual form-filling and document collection.
28+
Separate portals for central schemes
UMANG, DigiLocker, PM-JAY, PM Kisan, PM Awas — each with different logins, documents, and SLAs.
87%
Citizens unaware of their eligibility
Most rural and semi-urban Indians are unaware of which national schemes they qualify for.
The Solution

Five specialised AI agents,
working together for you.

1

You describe your situation

In any Indian language — text or voice. Tell Saarthi about a life event: a new baby, job loss, crop damage, illness, or anything else.

2

Eligibility Scout maps your benefits

Checks your profile against 1,600+ central and state schemes in seconds. Only shows you what you actually qualify for.

3

Document Orchestrator gathers & fills

Pulls documents from DigiLocker via ABDM. Auto-fills all form fields using Gemini Vision OCR. Flags only what's genuinely missing.

4

Submission Agent files on your behalf

Submits to the right government portal with zero copy-paste. Books CSC appointments when physical presence is required.

5

Tracker keeps you updated forever

Monitors status weekly, sends WhatsApp alerts in your language, and auto-files grievances if deadlines are missed.
